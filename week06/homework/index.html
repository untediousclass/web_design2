Everyone deserves a warm welcome

Whatever your knowledge of our products or brand is, you are a member of our community the moment you walk in the door and as such, you deserve a warm welcome from us. We work hard to provide that each time you visit.

You came to the right place

Whether you came in for a coffee roasted and brewed with craft, a plate of food to stifle your hunger or looking to pick up some of your favorite coffee beans online… if you’re at Copa Vida, you came to the right place. We will work hard to make you comfortable and help you get what you need.

Everything is intentional

From the bar height to the chair selection and the equipment we use, to the way we design our website and social media posts, everything we do is done with our community in mind. We see it as our job to think through the impact that our decisions have on your experience, and make them purposefully.
